Khuder Bhat

Spicy Broken Rice / Khuder Bhat /বউ খুদি / খুদের ভাত / বউয়া is typical Bengali delicacy. Khud(broken rice) is a grade of rice consisting of grains broken in the milling process.

Ingredients

  • 2 cup broken rice (khud)
  • Half cup sliced onion
  • 1 tablespoon sliced ginger
  • 1/3 cup oil (mustard oil preferable )
  • 3 cup hot water
  • Salt to taste.

Instructions

  1. Wash rice and place into a strainer. If you don’t have khud (broken rice), soak any Aromatic rice (still I prefer Bangladeshi chinigura or kalijeera rice) hour and break it with your hand to get your home made khud(broken rice).
  2. Heat the oil in pan and when oil ready, add onion, ginger, garlic and chilies pieces.
  3. Fry it 2 minutes, add hot water and cook it under closed lid.
  4. Serve Spicy Broken Rice / Khuder Bhat /বউ খুদি / খুদের ভাত / বউয়া with Bhorta, dal chochchori, dim bhaja(egg omlet) etc.
